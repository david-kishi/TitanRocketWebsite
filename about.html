About Us


The Titan Rocket Club was rebooted and established by a group of students at California State University, Fullerton in Spring 2020. Shortly afterwards, the club's senior research project team won CSUF's Engineering & Computer Science Research Project of the Year (2020-21).



The club was formed to bring together students interested in aerospace, specifically rockets.


Members are engaged with learning the basics of rocket science through collaborative projects and working towards rocket certifications with model rockets.
